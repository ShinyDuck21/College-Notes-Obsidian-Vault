## Basic Areas/Times
 - Founding - [[Civil War]] = very limited federal government, most governing done by states
 - [[Civil War]] - New Deal = Federal government grows in power at expense of states, real blending of federalism
 - New Deal - 1980s = federal government grows enormously in power, regulation of economy, expanding 'rights', most governing done by federal
 - 1980s - Today = somewhat of a push back to state sovereignty and limited federal government, but also push back on that push back
