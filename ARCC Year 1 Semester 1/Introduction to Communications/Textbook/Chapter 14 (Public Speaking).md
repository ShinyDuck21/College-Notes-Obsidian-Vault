## Understanding and Reducing Anxiety
---
Prevalence of Speech Anxiety
 - Speech anxiety affects nearly everyone
 - Famous figures even have experienced stage fright

Focus on Message over Self
 - Anxiety causes self-focus which is counterproductive.
 - Speakers should focus on their message's value to the audience

Practical Anxiety Management
 - Prepare for symptoms beforehand ex.
	 - Prepare a cough drop
	 - Have a bottle of water ready
	 - Take a walk

Channeling Nerves
 - Channel The fight or Flight response

## Delivery Modes and Preparation
---
Four Speech Delivery Modes
 - Manuscript (written)
 - Memorized
 - Impromptu
 - Extemporaneous (Structure but talk how normally talk)

Extemporaneous Benefits
 - Extemporaneous delivery balances structure with natural phrasing and enhancing audience connection

Importance of Preparation
 - Preparation, including memorizing key parts and practicing outlines, improves confidence and fluency

Memorization Techniques
 - Acronyms or Rhymes
 - Grouping
 - Loci

## Using Visual Aids and Media
---
Designing Effective Visual Aids includes
 - Avoid Text-Heavy Slides
	 - use minimal text to prevent overwhelming the audience and competing with speech
	 - Follow 6x6 rule for clarity
- Use High Contrast Fonts and Images
	- Ensure Fonts are readable with high contrast and include relevant images to complement spoken message
- Maintain Audience Engagement
	- Avoid turning back to read slides to keep engagement and vocal clarity
	- Face audience when presenting
- Optimize Remote Presentation Setup
	- Use proper camera angles, clean background and good microphone for clear presenting
